About Us

We make your old furniture look new again

Founded with a passion for craftsmanship and an unwavering commitment to transforming spaces, Upholstery Hub began its journey in South Africa. With over a decade of experience in revitalizing furniture, we have honed our skills to bring life back to the worn and the weary. Our dedication to exceptional quality and timeless design has been the cornerstone of our success.

As we expand our horizons, we are proud to bring our expertise to the beautiful landscapes of Rwanda and Zimbabwe. With the same fervor that ignited our inception, we embark on a new chapter, seeking to elevate homes, offices, and commercial establishments with our meticulous upholstery work.
